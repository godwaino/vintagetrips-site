Payments are made directly to travel service providers or through VintageTrips (acting as agent). Payment schedules vary by provider:

Each travel provider (hotel, train, tour operator) has its own cancellation policy. VintageTrips does not set these policies. Common terms include:

  • Heritage trains: Non-refundable or 25-50% cancellation fee if canceled 90-180 days before departure

We will clearly communicate all cancellation terms before you book.

5.2 VintageTrips Cancellations

If you cancel an itinerary request before any bookings are made, there is no charge. Once bookings are confirmed, provider cancellation policies apply.
