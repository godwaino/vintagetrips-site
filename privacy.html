Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

At VintageTrips, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

1. Data Controller

Company Name: VintageTrips
Contact Email: privacy@vintagetrips.com
Website: vintagetrips.com

We are the data controller responsible for your personal information. If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, please contact us using the details above.

2. What Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you request a custom itinerary or subscribe to our newsletter, we collect:

  • Contact details: Full name, email address, phone number (optional)
  • Travel preferences: Preferred destinations, travel dates, budget range, group size, interests
  • Additional information: Any details you voluntarily provide in free-text fields (e.g., special requirements, dietary needs, accessibility requirements)

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent on site, referral sources
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Cookies: See Section 7 for detailed cookie information

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Lawful Bases for Processing

We process your personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Contract performance: To provide you with custom itinerary planning services you've requested
  • Legitimate interests: To improve our services, send relevant travel content (where you've consented), and protect against fraud
  • Consent: For marketing communications (newsletter), which you can withdraw at any time
  • Legal obligation: To comply with tax, accounting, and other legal requirements

3.2 Purposes

We use your information to:

  • Create personalized travel itineraries based on your preferences
  • Communicate with you about your travel plans
  • Send booking confirmations and travel documents
  • Process payments (if booking through us)
  • Send newsletters and travel inspiration (only if you've opted in)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third parties who help us operate our business:

  • Email service providers: To send itineraries and newsletters (e.g., FormSubmit, Mailchimp)
  • Analytics providers: Google Analytics (anonymized data only)
  • Travel partners: Hotels, train operators, tour providers (only when booking services on your behalf, with your explicit consent)
  • Payment processors: To handle secure transactions (if applicable)

We never sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Itinerary requests: 24 months after your last interaction (or longer if you become a customer)
  • Newsletter subscribers: Until you unsubscribe
  • Customer data: Up to 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Analytics data: Anonymized and retained for up to 26 months (Google Analytics default)

After retention periods expire, we securely delete or anonymize your data.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & UK GDPR)

Under data protection law, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Right to restrict processing: Ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for marketing at any time (unsubscribe links in emails)

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, email us at: privacy@vintagetrips.com

Include "Data Subject Request" in the subject line. We'll respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies and Tracking

7.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us understand how you use our site and improve your experience.

7.2 Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to function (e.g., form submissions, session management)
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics (anonymized IP addresses) to understand traffic and improve content
  • Preference cookies: Remember your choices (e.g., currency preferences)

7.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit some website functionality. Learn how to manage cookies.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • SSL encryption (HTTPS) for data transmission
  • Secure form submission via FormSubmit
  • Access controls limiting who can view your information
  • Regular security reviews

While we take security seriously, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Data Transfers

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages (USA). Some third-party providers (e.g., Google Analytics, FormSubmit) may process data outside the UK/EU. Where data is transferred internationally, we ensure:

  • Adequate safeguards are in place (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses, Privacy Shield frameworks)
  • Providers comply with GDPR/UK GDPR requirements

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we've inadvertently collected such data, contact us immediately for deletion.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated via email to active users.

12. Complaints

If you're unhappy with how we've handled your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):

ICO Website: ico.org.uk
Helpline: 0303 123 1113

13. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, data access requests, or to exercise your rights:

Email: privacy@vintagetrips.com
Subject Line: "Privacy Enquiry" or "Data Subject Request"
Response Time: Within 30 days
