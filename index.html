FAQ

Do you customize trips?

Yes, every itinerary can be personalized by pace, style, and region. Our concierge team works with you to create the perfect vintage travel experience tailored to your preferences.

Is this premium-only?

Mostly premium, with selected mid-range curated options available. We believe in quality over quantity and handpick every accommodation and experience.

How far ahead should I book?

3-6 months is ideal for heritage rail and boutique properties. Popular routes like the Venice Simplon-Orient-Express can require even earlier booking.

What's included in your packages?

Our packages include accommodations, transportation, 24/7 concierge support, curated experiences, and detailed itineraries. Meals and optional activities can be added based on your preferences.

Do you offer group travel?

Yes! We can arrange intimate group experiences for 4-12 travelers, perfect for friends, families, or special celebrations.
